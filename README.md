# 🌿 MulchTracker

**MulchTracker** helps you track the cooldown of the item **“Enchanted Mulch”** (ID: 238388) across all your characters.

No more logging through alts to check cooldowns — everything in one place!

## 💬 Slash Commands

*   /mulch
*   /mulchtracker

***

## ✨ Features

📋 **Character Overview (Name-Realm)**  
Automatically lists all characters with **Herbalism**

🎨 **Class Colors**  
Each character is displayed in their **class color**

⏱️ **Cooldown Tracking**  
Shows exactly when your mulch will be ready again

✅ **Smart Status Colors**

🟢 Green = Ready  
🟡 Yellow = Less than 5 minutes remaining  
🔴 Red = On cooldown

🔽 **Sorted by Availability**  
Characters with the earliest ready time appear first

🚪 **Logout Button**  
Quickly switch characters directly from the addon

***

## ⚙️ How to Use

1.  Log in once on each character
    
2.  The addon will automatically detect:
    
    Herbalism profession  
    If the item was in your bags
    
3.  Cooldowns are then tracked account-wide
    

***
